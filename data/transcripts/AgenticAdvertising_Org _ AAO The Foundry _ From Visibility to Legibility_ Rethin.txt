Event: The Foundry by AgenticAdvertising.Org (AAO)

Title: From Visibility to Legibility: Rethinking Content in the Age of AI Discovery

Speakers: 

Lauren Price, SVP of Marketing, COS
Christina Cubeta, Founding Advisor, AgenticAdvertising.Org

AI referral traffic to U.S. retailers grew 760% year-over-year in late 2025 — and shoppers arriving via AI convert at higher rates than those coming from any other channel. The channel has arrived. But succeeding in it requires a fundamentally different kind of brand work. In this session, Lauren Price, SVP of Marketing & Communications at COS, shares how she has built one of the highest-performing brand presence scores among global retailers — ranking first for both brand value consistency and peer prioritization in AI-generated responses. 

The playbook isn't what most marketers expect: it centers on hero product clarity, answer-driven content, and a newly convergent role for PR, influencer, and editorial strategy as the infrastructure of AI discovery. Attendees will come away with a clear framework for optimizing simultaneously for machines and humans — and an understanding of why the brands that win in agentic commerce will be those that make themselves legible, not just visible.
