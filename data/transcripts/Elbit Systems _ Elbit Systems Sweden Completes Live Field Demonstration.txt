Over two weeks in Sweden, a large-scale live demonstration brought together soldiers, vehicles, sensors, and command posts into one unified digital network.
Across multiple scenarios, a 120-node field setup enabled real-time voice, video, and data sharing, from headquarters down to the tactical edge. 

Commanders maintained a continuous operational picture, while forces on the ground shared intelligence and executed missions with speed and coordination.
Built on a NATO interoperable architecture, the demonstration combined E-LynX™ SDR, Torch-X™ BMS and TIGER-X C2, showing how proven technologies come together to deliver resilient connectivity and effective command in complex environments.
