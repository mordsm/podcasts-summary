[SHOW NOTES]
In this project, I transformed a Chailiman 1583 beer can into a fully functional Bluetooth speaker.

Specifications:
- Dual JBL 4Ω 15W speakers
- Modified 30W Bluetooth amplifier board
- Samsung 29E 5000mAh battery pack (1S2P)
- USB Type-C charging port
- Power switch
- Recycled aluminum beer can enclosure

Despite its compact size, the sound quality is surprisingly powerful and clear.
