I Failed to Build a 12V Battery Spot Welder Timer... Any Ideas?

In this video, I attempted to build a timer-controlled spot welder using a 12V battery, a contactor, and a timer board based on the MOC3021 and BTA100A. After hours of testing, troubleshooting, rewiring, and experimenting with different solutions, I finally discovered that the circuit was designed for AC switching and couldn't properly control a DC relay coil.

I tried bypassing the timer, wiring components directly, and even testing alternative setups, but the results were disappointing.

Maybe I missed something. Maybe there's a better solution.

If you have experience building a timer for a 12V battery spot welder, I'd love to hear your suggestions in the comments.

Thanks for watching, and hopefully we'll find a solution together!
