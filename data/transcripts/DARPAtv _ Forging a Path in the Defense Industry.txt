DARPA Alum Amber Walker, Ph.D., shares her experiences transitioning from the agency and military service to the private sector. She highlights the invaluable role of teamwork and the exceptional support staff at DARPA, which fosters a culture of innovation and a powerful alumni network. Dr. Walker details her career progression through companies like Raytheon BBN and Anduril to her current role as Chief Growth Officer at Parry Labs, a digital systems integrator. She discusses how her time at DARPA equipped her to build a fulfilling career focused on delivering cutting-edge software and open-systems solutions to the warfighter.
