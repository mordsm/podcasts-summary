[SHOW NOTES]
Manuka is the most expensive honey in the world, and Beewise founder Saar Safra explains what justifies a price tag of up to a million dollars a kilo.

The manuka flower only grows in very specific places — high mountains in New Zealand and Australia, in exactly the right climate. To harvest it, crews fly hives in by helicopter, let the bees work, and lift them back out. Rare flower, punishing logistics, and a honey widely considered both the healthiest and the best-tasting on the market.
