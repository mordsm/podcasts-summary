GATR (Guided Advanced Tactical Rocket) is a combat-proven, laser-guided 70mm rocket designed for precision engagement across a wide range of battlefield scenarios.

Featuring Lock-On Before Launch (LOBL), a multi‑purpose penetrator warhead, programmable fuze delay, trajectory shaping, and enhanced accuracy of less than 1 meter CEP, GATR delivers reliable performance against static and moving targets - while minimizing collateral damage.
With an operational range of over 10 km, the ability to engage moving targets at speeds of up to 100 km/h, and proven use in operational environments worldwide, GATR is a mature, production‑ready solution for modern forces.
