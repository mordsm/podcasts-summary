How can soldiers securely communicate with and control unmanned systems on the battlefield?

E-LynX™ UxS enables seamless connectivity between soldiers, unmanned aerial systems (UAS) and ground platforms, forming a unified tactical network.

Built on Elbit Systems’ Software Defined Radio technology, it supports voice, data, video and control over a resilient MANET environment, enhancing coordination, extending range and improving situational awareness in complex operations.
