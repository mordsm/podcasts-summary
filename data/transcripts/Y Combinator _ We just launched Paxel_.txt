[SHOW NOTES]
Paxel is a free tool that helps you understand how you build with AI coding agents.

It analyzes your Claude, Codex, and Cursor sessions and gives you a builder profile: how you plan, how you steer agents, how you execute, and what patterns show up across your work.

It runs locally inside Docker, so your code never leaves your machine. Run one command from your repo and your profile lands in your inbox.

If you’re applying to Startup School, paste your Paxel token into your application. Adding it can only help, never hurt. Already applied? You can still go back and add your token.

We want to understand how you build.
