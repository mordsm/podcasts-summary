Applied FinOps is a podcast for practitioners working through the messy, real-world version of FinOps inside complex organizations.

In this episode, Amit Kinha and Frank Contrepois unpack new research on how finance leaders are managing AI spend, budget overruns, governance, and the pressure to prove returns quickly.

They explore the AI FinOps maturity paradox: why organizations that consider themselves more mature in FinOps are often reporting the highest AI overruns. The conversation examines whether those companies are actually less in control, or simply have enough visibility to see the problem clearly while others are still operating with blind spots.

Amit and Frank also discuss AI budgeting, token usage, cost attribution, operational efficiency versus growth, accountability across finance and technology, and why organizations need to connect AI spend to business outcomes rather than treating it as a standalone cost line.

This is a conversation for FinOps, finance, technology, procurement, and operations leaders trying to build meaningful controls around AI without slowing down the experimentation that may create the most value.
