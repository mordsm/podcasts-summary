We combined reSpeaker Flex with the reBot Arm B601 to build an intelligent voice-driven robotic interaction system powered by:
✅ DOA sound source tracking
✅ Voice command control
✅ Whisper speech recognition
✅ Llama-3.3 intent understanding
✅ Real-time robotic motion control

reSpeaker Flex is a versatile 4-mic array designed specifically for robots and embodied AI applications. Powered by XMOS XVF3800, it features on-board AI acoustic algorithms such as AEC, beamforming, and Noise Suppression.
With a split design and options for circular or linear mic arrays, it supports both dual modes(I2S/USB), making it the smart ear for embodied AI projects requiring far-field and clear voice capture.

Now the reBot arm can:
👂 Detect where your voice comes from
🧠 Understand natural language commands
🤖 Turn, nod, wave, and interact in real time
