Many marketers still think click fraud is overhyped.

It is not.

Our CMO, Chad Kinlay, explains why click fraud is one of the biggest hidden drains in digital advertising, with advertisers losing 10 to 20 percent of budgets to bots, navigational traffic, hyper-engaged users, and other non-genuine clicks that deliver no real outcome.
