Event: The Foundry by AgenticAdvertising.Org (AAO)

Title: Don't Let It Happen to You: Why Marketers Must Own the Technology Shaping Their Future

Speakers: Brian O’Kelley, Founding Architect, AgenticAdvertising.Org

The programmatic era taught us a hard lesson: when marketers step back from the technology, the technology moves forward without them. Brian O'Kelley draws a direct line from that cautionary history to the moment we're in now, where the protocols and standards underlying agentic advertising are being written in real time. This session makes the case that you don't need to be an engineer to have a seat at this table — but you do need to show up. Brian will lay out why AAO exists to serve both the technical architects and the orchestrating marketers of the future, and why understanding the infrastructure isn't optional for anyone who wants to lead through this transformation.
