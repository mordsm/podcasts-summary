In this project, I transformed a wooden wine barrel into a unique DIY Bluetooth speaker.

Specifications:
• TB21 Bluetooth amplifier board (TPA3116D2)
• 2 x 4Ω 50W full-range speakers
• 1 x 4Ω 40W sub/mid speaker
• 4-cell 32800 LiFePO4 battery pack
• 4S 200A BMS
• External volume control

The classic wooden barrel design combined with modern electronics creates a powerful and stylish portable speaker system with impressive sound quality.
