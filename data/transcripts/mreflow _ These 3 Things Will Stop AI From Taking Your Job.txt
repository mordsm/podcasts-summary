If you want to prevent AI from taking your job in the future, these are my top 3 pieces of advice:

1. Fall in love with learning. It’s one of the most common trait amongst successful people. 2. Build stuff. Companies don't just want book smarts anymore, they want people who can build in the real world. Yes, build with AI, but also understanding how things actually work too.
3. Get good at talking to people. This will be the ultimate competitive advantage in an AI world. A lot of people will lose their social skills from spending too much time online and connecting with bots and people through a screen. Don’t be one of them.
