This session examines the world of malicious insider threat by identifying the trends and patterns of the Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures (TTPs) observed in over 1,000 cases. Rather than focus on attitudinal surveys or anecdotal data, this session will explore the TTPs used by malicious insiders which are most valuable to digital forensic examiners and incident responders.
