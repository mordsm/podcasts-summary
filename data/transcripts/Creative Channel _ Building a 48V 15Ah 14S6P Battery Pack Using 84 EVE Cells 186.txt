Building a 48V 15Ah 14S6P Battery Pack Using 84 EVE Cells for Electric Bikes

In this video, I build a complete 48V 15Ah 14S6P battery pack using 84 reclaimed EVE 26V cells with a real capacity of around 2.55Ah per cell. This battery setup is compatible with about 99% of common electric bicycles.

Video includes:
- Building the 14S6P battery configuration
- Installing the BMS and 48V 15Ah battery case
- Voltage checking and real-world testing
- Testing on 3 different electric bikes:
• 133S electric bike
• Espero E-SYNC
• Moka electric bicycle
