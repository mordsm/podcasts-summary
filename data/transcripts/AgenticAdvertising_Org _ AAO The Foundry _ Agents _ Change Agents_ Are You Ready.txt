Event: The Foundry by AgenticAdvertising.Org (AAO)

Title: Agents & Change Agents: Are You Ready for the Future of Marketing?

Speakers: 

Randall Rothenberg, Advisor, AgenticAdvertising.Org
Matthew Egol, Advisor, AgenticAdvertising.Org 

Agentification is fueling the rebundling of the marketing supply chain, transforming marketers from buyers of services into orchestrators of value creation. In this session, Randall Rothenberg and Matthew Egol examine how this structural shift is already delivering massive gains in efficiency and organizational speed. They will explore the transition from siloed vendors to integrated agentic ecosystems and challenge leadership to answer the definitive question of the era: Is your company ready? Attendees will gain a roadmap for navigating this rebundling and a clear view of the competitive advantages awaiting those who lead the charge.
