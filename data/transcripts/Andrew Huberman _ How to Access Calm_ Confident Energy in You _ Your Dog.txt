[SHOW NOTES]
Cesar Millan is a world-renowned dog behavior expert.

While this episode is about human-dog relationships (and cats are mentioned as well), it's really about human energy and how to attain and maintain a calm, assertive, and confident energy for all relationships — both with dogs and with humans.

Cesar shares specific tools for returning home to your dog each day, structuring their walks, and eliminating aggressive behaviors and anxiety.

We discuss how to determine whether your dog is front, middle, or back of the pack order — a hardwired feature that determines much of their needs and personality.

And we explore how human-human interactions are based on balances in energy, too.

Both dog owners and non-owners will gain extremely valuable insights and tools from this episode.
