For our May Ranger Training, we go deeper into Spatial & Energy Management at the L3 level. This session is designed to help trainees move beyond simply “being able to build” toward becoming professionals who can independently maintain, troubleshoot, and develop complete systems.

The training focus on:
1) Building a deeper understanding of HA system architecture, boot processes, service management, and logging systems
2) Developing structured troubleshooting and diagnostic skills for common system issues
3) Learning backup, recovery, version upgrade, and security hardening workflows
4) Using Node-RED to create more advanced business logic and third-party integrations
5) Adapting to new requirements, including hardware integration and protocol support
6) Understanding when and how to collaborate efficiently with headquarters for escalations and system upgrades
