[SHOW NOTES]
Most teams reach for better prompts when agents break. AutonomyAI reaches for the harness. This talk introduces harness engineering as the discipline of making mistakes structurally impossible, then walks through how AutonomyAI runs two harnesses in parallel: an internal R&D lab where patterns are tested against real evals, and a product harness that personalizes per customer codebase. The two are connected by a flywheel: every customer trace sharpens the lab, every lab experiment ships to every customer. When models plateau, harnesses compound.
