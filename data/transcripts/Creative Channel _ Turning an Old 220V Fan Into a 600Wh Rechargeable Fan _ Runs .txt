In this project, I converted an old 220V fan into a rechargeable battery-powered fan.

🔹 Nidec 30W BLDC motor
🔹 14.6V 4S3P Lifepo4 battery pack
🔹 12x 32140 LiFePO4 cells
🔹 45Ah capacity (~600Wh)
🔹 Adjustable speed control
🔹 Battery level indicator
🔹 Up to 20 hours of runtime

This DIY upgrade gives a broken fan a second life, making it portable, energy-efficient, and perfect for power outages, camping, or outdoor use.
