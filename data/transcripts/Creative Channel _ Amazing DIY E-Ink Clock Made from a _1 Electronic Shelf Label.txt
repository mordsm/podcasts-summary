[SHOW NOTES]
I bought a cheap 2.13-inch E-Ink display that was originally used as an Alibaba Electronic Shelf Label (ESL).

After flashing custom firmware and designing several 3D printed enclosures, I transformed it into a collection of beautiful low-power smart clocks.

The display consumes power only when updating the screen, allowing a tiny lithium battery to power it for months or even more than a year.

Features:
• 2.13-inch E-Ink display
• 250 × 122 resolution
• Bluetooth connectivity
• Ultra-low power consumption
• Lunar calendar support
• Temperature display
• Custom 3D printed cases

A fun DIY project that combines electronics, firmware, and 3D printing.
