[SHOW NOTES]
Meet Mike Spicer (aka DarkMatter), a NOC lead at Black Hat, revealing how the team detected and tracked down a compromised attendee during the conference.

When a device connected to the network and started communicating with a known malicious source, an alert was triggered among hundreds of thousands of events. The team conducted a deep dive analysis, examining packet types and communication patterns to identify the threat actor through behavioral analysis. Using open-source intelligence techniques, the team fingerprinted the network communication, pieced together the digital breadcrumbs, and matched the activity to a registered attendee. The team successfully made contact to help secure the compromised device.
